How it works
  1. Tax profile — entity type, legal name, EIN, mailing address (form below).
  2. Books review — ask the AI assistant to "review the accounting for the tax year"; it runs the full protocol (transfers, duplicates, credit cards) before any numbers are used.
  3. Pull forms — the assistant downloads the official IRS PDFs it determines you need (e.g. Schedule C, 4562, 1099-NEC).
  4. Complete — the assistant fills the forms from your ledger, line by line.
  5. Approve — you review each filled PDF below and click Approve. Nothing can be mailed without this.
  6. Mail — the assistant (or you) mails approved forms via Lob, certified by default, with delivery tracking.
Start by telling the AI assistant: "prepare my {{ nav.active_company_name }} taxes".
